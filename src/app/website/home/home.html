Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ShopBot

How does the 30-day free trial work?

Start using ShopBot immediately with full access to all features. No credit card required. After 30 days, choose a plan that fits your property size. You can cancel anytime during the trial.

Can I switch plans later?

Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any billing differences.

Do you offer training and onboarding support?

Yes! All plans include access to our knowledge base and video tutorials. Professional and higher plans include personalized onboarding sessions with our customer success team.

Is my data secure?

Security is our top priority. We use industry-standard encryption, regular backups, and comply with GDPR and other international data protection regulations. Your data is hosted on secure cloud infrastructure with 99.9% uptime.

Can I integrate with my existing systems?

ShopBot integrates seamlessly with popular OTAs, payment gateways, and channel managers. Enterprise and Premium plans include API access for custom integrations with your existing tools.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for annual plans. All payments are processed securely through Stripe.
